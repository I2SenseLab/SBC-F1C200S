\section*{\textit{Bill of materials}}

The complete bill of materials is provided in the project repository as \texttt{Hardware/sbcBOM.csv}. The reference designators in the bill of materials match the designators used in the schematic and PCB layout files. For readability, repeated passive components with the same package and supplier part number are grouped in the summary table below. The full editable CSV file should be used for ordering, assembly, and detailed component-level verification.

\vskip 0.2cm

\noindent
The hardware is primarily assembled from commercially available surface-mount components selected for compatibility with low-cost PCB fabrication and assembly services. The complete bill of materials includes the manufacturer or supplier part numbers, datasheet links where available, footprints, reference designators, and JLCPCB/LCSC part numbers. Costs are intentionally reported through the editable BOM and fabrication quotations because component pricing and availability vary over time, particularly for semiconductor devices and PCB assembly services.
